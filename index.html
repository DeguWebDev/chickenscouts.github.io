Greatshell Wrangling

Issued in conjunction with the release of The Way of Kings leatherbound Kickstarter campaign, this patch shows your efforts to learn how to manage and control greatshells. Use your mind and scheming for fun and profit!

How to Obtain:

  • Back The Way of Kings 10th anniversary leatherbound on Kickstarter.
  • Available for purchase in the Dragonsteel Store.

Shardblade Safety

How to Obtain:

  • Available for purchase in the Dragonsteel Store.
